Frequently
Asked Questions

Get answers to your questions about giving, funding, and making a real impact.

What can I give to?

You can contribute to verified Zakat recipients, Sadaqah causes (like food aid, education, and health), or Waqf projects such as mosques, schools, and long-term community development, all following Shariah guidelines.

Is there a minimum or maximum amount I can give?

There is no fixed limit. Any amount you give with intention counts and helps those in need.

Can I donate to more than one cause?

Yes, you can contribute to multiple causes as per your preference and capacity.

When will the funds reach the selected cause?

Funds are processed promptly and reach the intended cause within a few working days.

Will my donation be fully received by the cause I selected?

Yes, we ensure transparency and full disbursement to the selected cause following our guidelines.
